Introduction

Naqsh

Naqsh is an editorial page focused on the influence of Persian art and design across fashion, architecture, and branding. "Naqsh" means pattern or imprint in Farsi. Just like a pattern, Persian design has left its mark everywhere. This page is here to trace that mark, celebrate the culture behind it, and give credit where it's long overdue.
